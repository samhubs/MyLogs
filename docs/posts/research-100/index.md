## Entry Format

Every paper implementation should answer the same core questions:

- What problem does the paper solve?
- What is the central idea?
- What did I implement?
- Which results did I reproduce?
- What broke, surprised me, or changed my understanding?
- What reusable engineering lesson came out of it?
